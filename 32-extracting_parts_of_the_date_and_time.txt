32 - Extracting parts of the date and time

Notes
    * .getDay() - returns a number in the range of (0-6).
    * .getMonth() - (0-11)
    * .getDate() - (1-31)
    * .getFullYear() - returns a 4-digit number for the year.
    * .getHours() - (0-23)
    * .getSeconds() - (0-59)
    * .getMilliseconds() - (0-999)
    * .getTime() - gives you the number of milliseconds that have elapsed since midnight, Jan. 1, 1970.
