65 - The DOM: Attributes

Notes
    * <elment something="something in quotes">
        * An element with attribute.
            * The equal sign and the quotes are the tipoff.
        * The word on the left of the equal sign is the attribute name.
        * The word on the right side of the equal sign is the attribute value.
    * An element can have any number of attributes.
    * .hasAttribute("class") returns either true or false.
    * .getAttribute("class") reads the value of an attribute.
    * .setAttribute("class", "special") sets the value of an attribute.
